OFFICE REMOVALS

TPC MOVERS team have the expertise and flexibility to handle every aspect of your office move efficiently and with minimum downtime for your business.

No matter whether you are moving to a bigger office across town, relocating to another part of Cyprus or even relocating abroad – we provide a comprehensive office removals service that can be tailored to suit your individual requirements.

NATIONAL REMOVALS

Whether you are moving to same city or another city, your dedicated move coordinator will organise the packing, loading, transit, unloading and delivery of your belongings through our in-house teams . You just have to tell us what you want to take, and we take care of the rest.

Man and Van

Man & Van is the best value, most flexible, fastest man and van service operating in Cyprus. We have built a great reputation thanks to our high standard of customer service. Over many years of relocating homes and businesses to and from every part of Cyprus, we’ve proved ourselves dependable and affordable.

Our aim is to take the stress out of your relocation. We have a fleet of vehicles and equipment to ensure that there is no job we can’t undertake with our man and a van service.

We charge by the hour for our man and van services, which means that you won’t pay for anything more than you need to. If necessary, we will send a job-specific surveyor out to give you an accurate quotation and we can provide as many men and vehicles as the job requires. We have a range of vehicles ready for any sized job. We can also move you at short notice.
